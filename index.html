Welcome to KenyaFund

Step 1: Sign Up

Register an account with us. It's quick and easy. Just click on the "Sign Up" link at the top of the page.

Step 2: Start a Fundraiser or Donate

Once you have an account, you can start your own fundraiser or donate to existing ones. Click on "Start a Fundraiser" to begin your own campaign, or "Explore Campaigns" to see what others are doing.

Step 3: Share & Connect

Share your fundraiser with your network or connect with others who are passionate about the same causes. Your contribution can make a big difference.
